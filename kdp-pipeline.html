● The Market Analyst

The Market Analyst ensures that every project has a commercial target. It performs deep-dive research into current trends and Amazon KDP categories.

  • Identifies high-traffic, low-competition keywords.
  • Analyzes competitor books for tone, length, and pricing strategy.
  • Validates the request/book-idea.md against market demand.
  • Suggests optimal KDP categories and sub-genres.

● The Architect

The Architect is responsible for the skeletal structure of the book. It takes the initial request and transforms it into a viable commercial outline.

  • Analyzes requests/book-idea.md for tone and target audience.
  • Generates a comprehensive outline.json in the project folder.
  • Defines chapter goals, key takeaways, and narrative arcs.
  • Ensures logical flow and professional non-fiction/fiction pacing.

● The Ghostwriter

The workhorse of the factory. The Ghostwriter takes the Architect's outline and expands each chapter into full-length prose.

  • Writes chapter-by-chapter based on the outline.json.
  • Maintains consistent character voices (fiction) or authority (non-fiction).
  • Produces chapter-X-draft.md files for every section.
  • Focuses on word count targets and technical accuracy.

● The Humanizer

The final gatekeeper of tone. The Humanizer reviews the Ghostwriter's drafts to remove "AI-isms" and inject personality, warmth, and engagement.

  • Refines sentence structure for better flow and rhythm.
  • Adds sensory details, idioms, and natural conversational patterns.
  • Produces the chapter-X-FINAL.md versions.
  • Prepares the manuscript for final QA review.

● The Reviewer

The final quality control agent. The Reviewer performs a comprehensive check of the entire manuscript before it reaches the output folder.

  • Verifies consistency across all chapters and character arcs.
  • Checks for adherence to KDP formatting and compliance standards.
  • Finalizes the MANUSCRIPT_RAW.md for compilation.
  • Generates the KDP_SUBMISSION_INFO.txt metadata file.
